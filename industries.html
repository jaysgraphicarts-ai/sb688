32GB braided card use

The 32GB card can work as a branded physical access and portable business packet. It can hold demo files, onboarding packets, public pages, QR routes, exports, and encrypted customer files. The safest design is to keep live secrets server-side and use the card as a key plus portable packet.

CARD CAN HOLD:
- public demo materials
- industry sample packets
- QR/access route
- public card ID
- encrypted exports
- brand/media files

CARD SHOULD NOT HOLD UNPROTECTED:
- raw passwords
- service role keys
- unencrypted private memory
- full client database
- payment secrets
