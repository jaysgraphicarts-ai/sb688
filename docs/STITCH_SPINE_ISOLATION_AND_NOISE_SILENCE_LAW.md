# JGA Stitch Hive Mind — Spine Isolation and Unverifiable Noise Silence Law

## Core Law

```text
Nothing touches the Spine.
Unverifiable outside noise is silenced.
No active state becomes trusted state without verification.
```

## Purpose

The Spine is the protected truth rail of every Stitch system, Business Brick, Micro-Chip, tenant runtime, state compliance agent, tax agent, memory braid, AI connector, and Owner's Room session.

Nothing touches the Spine directly.

All outside noise that cannot be verified must be silenced, quarantined, or rejected before it can influence decisions, memory, policy, automation, records, compliance, tax logic, billing logic, or owner-facing reports.

## Spine Definition

The Spine protects:

- core laws;
- owner authority;
- trusted checkpoints;
- verified ledger head;
- trusted memory lineage;
- emergency cutoff authority;
- tenant isolation rules;
- compliance boundaries;
- tax responsibility boundaries;
- Brick verification rules;
- AI truth boundary;
- subscription enforcement law;
- recordkeeping integrity law.

## No-Touch Rule

The following may not directly modify the Spine:

- tenants;
- Business Bricks;
- Mini-Chips;
- Micro-Chips;
- local AI models;
- external AI systems;
- uploaded files;
- web scans;
- compliance agents;
- tax agents;
- billing webhooks;
- prompts;
- plugin/tool outputs;
- automated fixers;
- background jobs.

All changes that could affect Spine-level law must pass through Owner's Room review, verification, ledgering, and tie-off.

## Noise Handling Outcomes

Every outside signal must resolve to one outcome:

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---|---|
| SILENCE | ignored, no influence allowed |
| QUARANTINE | held for review |
| REJECT | blocked as unsafe or unauthorized |
| REDACT | sensitive or irrelevant content removed |
| VERIFY | source check required |
| ESCALATE | owner/human review required |
| LEARN_SAFE | sanitized, verified reusable lesson allowed |

## Silence Rule

If a signal cannot be verified, scoped, sourced, or tied to an authorized actor, it must not influence:

- the Spine;
- trusted memory;
- tenant records;
- compliance reports;
- tax summaries;
- subscription status;
- emergency state;
- AI doctrine;
- Brick configuration;
- Micro-Chip manifests;
- owner dashboards;
- audit reports.

Unverified content may be stored only as quarantined evidence with clear status.

## AI Agent Boundary

Any AI may request connection, but no AI may touch the Spine.

AI outputs are treated as proposals unless verified.

AI may assist with summarizing, drafting, organizing, scanning, comparing, reporting, and suggesting fixes.

AI may not rewrite core laws, override owner authority, promote memory to trusted state, bypass emergency cutoff, alter tenant isolation, certify compliance, certify tax accuracy, silence audit logs, or self-authorize a Spine change.

## Brick and Micro-Chip Boundary

Business Bricks and Micro-Chips operate beside the Spine, under policy.

They may not operate inside the Spine.

If a Brick or chip attempts to modify core law, tenant isolation, owner authority, or ledger integrity, it must be frozen and reviewed.

## Owner's Room Control

Only the Owner's Room can consider Spine-level changes.

Owner's Room must require:

1. clear change request;
2. reason for change;
3. affected systems;
4. risk classification;
5. verification proof;
6. rollback plan;
7. ledger entry;
8. tie-off checkpoint.

## Emergency Override

Emergency cutoff can freeze all automation, Bricks, chips, AI agents, compliance agents, tax agents, and subscriptions.

Emergency cutoff still does not allow unverified outside noise to touch the Spine.

## Final Rule

```text
The Spine is protected.
Noise is silenced.
Only verified truth moves forward.
```
